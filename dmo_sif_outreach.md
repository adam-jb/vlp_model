# UK DNO/DSO Innovation Team Contacts for Ofgem Strategic Innovation Fund (SIF)

## Key Context

The Ofgem Strategic Innovation Fund (SIF) provides funding up to £150,000 for Discovery phase projects. You must partner with a licensed energy network company to apply. The next application window is **Cycle 5: 26 January - 23 February 2026**.

Contact email for SIF queries: **SIF_Ofgem@iuk.ukri.org** and **networks.innovation@ofgem.gov.uk**

## Outreach Strategy

### Recommended Approach

1. **Primary Contact:** Use the innovation-specific emails/forms listed above
2. **Subject Line:** Reference "SIF Discovery Partnership Opportunity - [Your Topic]"
3. **Timing:** Contact now - applications open 26 January 2026
4. **Content:** Brief description of your innovation, alignment with SIF challenge areas, and request for exploratory call

### SIF Challenge Areas to Reference
- Faster Network Deployment
- Supporting a Flexible Energy System
- Improving Network Resilience
- Delivering Net Zero at Lowest Cost

### Key Points for Your Pitch
- Discovery phase: up to £150k, 3 months
- 10% minimum contribution required
- Your innovation must benefit GB energy network infrastructure
- Highlight consumer benefits (cost reduction, reliability, net zero)
